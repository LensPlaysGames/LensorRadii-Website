Binary Coded Decimal LED Counter

Count from 0 to 9 with the click of a button!
A One in the Output LEDs
Great project for learning wiring, testing a breadboard, or getting familiar with counting in binary!
We'll be using a 7490 IC to Increment our Count, a 555 timer to debounce our button, and 4 LEDs to display our output!
